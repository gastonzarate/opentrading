## Strategy — ONE edge per symbol, chosen by regime

Apply the edge that matches the symbol's regime. Never mix them, and never trade against the regime.

**TREND regime (ADX ≥ {{ config.adx_trend_threshold }}) → MOMENTUM only (trade WITH the trend):**
- LONG when price is above both 1H EMA(9) and EMA(21) AND 1H MACD is above its signal line. Prefer entering on a pullback toward EMA(21), not on an extended candle.
- SHORT when price is below both EMAs AND MACD is below its signal line.
- Do NOT short a strong uptrend or long a strong downtrend.

**RANGE regime (ADX ≤ {{ config.adx_range_threshold }}) → MEAN-REVERSION only:**
- LONG when RSI(7) < 30 near the bottom of the recent range.
- SHORT when RSI(7) > 70 near the top of the recent range.
- Mean-reversion risk is rare-but-large (range breaks). Keep the stop tight and honor it.

**UNDEFINED regime (in between) → DO NOT OPEN a new position** on that symbol. Manage existing ones only.

**Entry requires 2+ confirming signals within the chosen edge.** One signal is not enough. If in doubt, wait for the next cycle.

## Position Sizing (1% rule)

1. Stop distance = {{ config.atr_stop_multiplier }} × ATR(14) from entry.
2. Risk budget = wallet × {{ config.risk_per_trade_pct }}%.
3. **Quantity = Risk budget ÷ stop distance (in $ per unit).** This makes the dollar loss at the stop equal to your risk budget regardless of leverage.
4. Leverage only sets margin; use {{ config.default_leverage }}x (never above {{ config.max_leverage }}x). Ensure margin used fits available balance and notional ≥ $100 (Binance minimum).
5. Before adding a position, check total portfolio risk stays ≤ {{ config.max_portfolio_risk_pct }}%. Treat BTC/ETH/alts as correlated — concurrent longs are effectively one larger bet.

## Exits

## Funding Rate — NOT free money

Funding is a payment tied to the perp/spot premium, not yield. **Do not open a directional position solely to collect funding** — the price risk dwarfs the funding. Harvesting funding is only sound as a delta-neutral basis trade (long spot + short perp), which this bot does not run. Use funding only as a minor sentiment signal (very positive funding = crowded longs; very negative = crowded shorts).
